History

Farid Group is a leading rope and net manufacturing company in Bangladesh. We have been manufacturing PE /PP rope since 1978. We have been leading the local market and exporting our products with Japanese and Germany technology without any complain. Day by day new products are being included with higher technology in our product list. Now we are Manufacturing and Leading the local market with our PE rope/PP rope/Danline Rope/polyester multifilament rope/Jute rope/Tire cord rope /polyester multifilament net and PE twine net. In 1978 the company started its slow step by manufacturing 1,000 kg/day for the time being, now our manufacturing capacity has reached to 20,000 kg/day. Group believes in customer's satisfaction and sincerity of 2,000 trained workers & over 100 experienced employees has reached the company in today's position. The company is Certified by ISO (9001/2000) for it's quality management system. Our main products are Rope- (PP/PE/Danline/Nylon multifilament/Tire cord/Jute & Lead rope) for Fishing, Marine and Construction use. Nylon/Polyester multifilament net for fishing only. PE twines net for Fencing/Agro shade/Construction/Poultry/Bird protecting/Fisheries farm.
